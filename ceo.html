ABOUT THE FOUNDER

Visionary Leader in Global Media & Data

Yejung Lee is a broadcast communicator, industry journalist, and media entrepreneur dedicated to building a global intelligence platform that connects Korean innovation with international markets. With over a decade of experience in media, journalism, and digital transformation, she founded K-IMPACT Media Group to create a data-driven ecosystem that empowers businesses and creators worldwide.

Her mission is to transform how the world perceives Korean products, culture, and innovation by providing real-time market intelligence, editorial curation, and strategic insights. Through K-IMPACT Media Group and its subsidiaries including K-Beauty, she is pioneering a new model of media platforms that combine content, data, and commerce.

Core Philosophy: "Content moves people. Structure changes the world." This principle guides all initiatives at K-IMPACT, emphasizing the importance of meaningful storytelling backed by robust data infrastructure and strategic business frameworks.
