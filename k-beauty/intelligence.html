GROWTH DRIVER
North America K-Beauty Boom
The US market is driving the largest K-Beauty growth globally, with TikTok and Instagram accelerating consumer discovery and purchase conversion at unprecedented rates.
US K-Beauty market grew 28% YoY in 2026

TREND
Ingredient-First Consumer Behavior
Global consumers are increasingly ingredient-literate, driving demand for K-Beauty's science-backed, ingredient-focused formulations. Snail mucin, centella, and niacinamide lead global searches.
"Snail mucin" searches up 340% globally (2024-2026)
Centella asiatica products grew 180% in US
Niacinamide remains #1 searched skincare ingredient
Clean beauty + K-Beauty overlap growing rapidly
MARKET SHIFT
Indie Brand Disruption
Indie K-Beauty brands like COSRX, Anua, and Beauty of Joseon are outpacing traditional conglomerates in global growth, driven by strong social media engagement and direct-to-consumer strategies.
Indie brands now represent 45% of K-Beauty exports
Average indie brand growth rate: 120% YoY
P&G acquired COSRX for $1.7B in 2023
M&A activity in K-Beauty accelerating

2030 Market Projection
The global K-Beauty market is projected to reach $43.5B by 2030, driven by North America, Europe, and Southeast Asia expansion. Sunscreen and serum categories will lead growth.
